# Project Mnemosyne

A private, local-first digital brain for Alex and Mimi.

---

## Priorities

1. Reliability
2. Privacy
3. Longevity
4. Maintainability
5. Explainability
6. Intelligence

---

## Principles

### Models are replaceable.

Memories are not.

### Never fabricate memories.

Distinguish:

- Known facts
- Retrieved memories
- Assumptions

### Simplicity beats complexity.

### Every subsystem should survive independently.

### Tools are optional.

### Privacy is absolute.

### Documentation is part of the system.

### Human approval precedes permanent memory.
